We blended bodybuilding with high-intensity interval training to deliver a bigger, leaner body in 21 days.When you work your heart at this rate on a regular basis two things happen. First, you’ll be strengthening the left ventricle of your heart, and thickening its walls. As a result of this strengthening, you’ll be increasing your heart’s stroke volume—the amount of blood that’s pumped with each heartbeat. When a single beat of your heart pumps more blood, your body works more efficiently—and more oxygen is spread throughout your body.
